Reading under real conditions

Contrast that looks correct on a calibrated panel can behave very differently on a cheap laptop in a bright room. This paragraph is set at body size so you can judge the pair the way you would actually meet it: sustained reading, not a glance at a swatch. Stay with it for a moment and notice whether the edges of the letters feel stable or whether the page itself starts to press back.

Spot text and captions render at this size — often the first thing to go.
